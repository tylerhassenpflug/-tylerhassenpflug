Interests

My personal interests include:

The last one may or may not have resulted with a trip to the ER in rural Thailand but that's a story for another time.
  • Mountaineering, Rock Climbing and Ice Climbing. I live in the Pacific Northwest, home of some of the greatest mountains and trails in the country. I enjoy spending weekends on scrambles and getting lost in the woods.
